Welcome to Makkah Clock Royal Tower, A Fairmont Hotel

About us:

Makkah Clock Royal Tower A Fairmont Hotel is a beacon for pilgrims in the heart of the Holy City. It overlooks al-Kaaba and al-Masjid al-Haram, making it the closest accommodation to the Kaaba and the city’s best hotel for Umrah and Hajj. As the focal point of the Clock Towers Complex, the tower offers 76 floors of exquisitely designed luxury, including 1,650 rooms and suites, many with sweeping views of Makkah’s revered religious sites. Choose a Fairmont Gold room or stay on the Fairmont Royal Floor for the most luxurious experience imaginable.Makkah Clock Royal Tower, A Fairmont Hotel features 9 state-of-the-art dining venues, including the Indian-inspired Bharat, the Lebanese lounge Al Dar and the rooftop restaurant Al Dira. The hotel also offers a comprehensive 24-hour room service menu. Guests can enjoy secretarial service, meeting rooms with an event area and a very spacious ballroom for meetings and events. A media room with live streaming capabilities is also available. The hotel is part of the Abraj Al Bait complex, which houses 4,000 shops and stores, and offers a full range of international brands. Makkah Clock Royal Tower, A Fairmont Hotel is just a 2-minute walk from the Holy Mosque and 6 km from Hira Cave.
